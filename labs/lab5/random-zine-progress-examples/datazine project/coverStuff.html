One week of my life depicted in compliments


Throughout the week I took notice and wrote down all of the compliments people gave me and the compliments I gave them. I wrote down the day, the person who gave me the compliment (or to whom I gave the compliment), the compliment type and the medium in which I received or gave the compliment. Based on my data set I decided to categorize my compliments if 4 categories - work, appearance, personality, and birthday related. Explained on this cover page is the key to reading my collected data. After this project I realized that I receive many more compliments than I give (even after taking into account that my birthday was on Sunday, which explains the huge amount of compiments I recieved then), which I have promised to myself to change and start giving people more compliments.
